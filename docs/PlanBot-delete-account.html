Request Account Deletion

At PlanBot, we respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so quickly and securely via the app, or by contacting our support team if you no longer have access.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent. This action will:
  • Permanently delete your profile and personal information.
  • Erase all your study plans, messages, and progress history.
  • Cancel any active subscriptions (via Google Play management).

Method 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your account instantly.

  1. Open the PlanBot App.
  2. Go to Settings (⚙️ icon).
  3. Tap on Profile.
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  5. Confirm your choice by typing "DELETE".

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app or have uninstalled it, you can request account deletion by email. We will process your request within 7 days.

Please email us from the email address associated with your PlanBot account:

Email Support for Deletion

Email: plannexttech@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Data Retention Policy

Once your account is deleted, your data is removed from our active databases immediately. Backups are purged in accordance with our retention cycles (typically within 30 days).
