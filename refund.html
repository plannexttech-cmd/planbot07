Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

Important: All purchases in PlanBot are processed through Google Play. Refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policy and terms.

1. Google Play Refund Policy

Since all PlanBot subscriptions are purchased through Google Play, refund requests must be made through Google Play, not directly to us.

1.1 Google Play's 48-Hour Window

Google Play offers a 48-hour refund window for most purchases:

  • Refunds requested within 48 hours of purchase are typically approved automatically
  • After 48 hours, refunds are at Google's discretion
  • You can request a refund directly from the Google Play Store app or website

2. How to Request a Refund

Method 1: Through Google Play App

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap the Menu icon (☰) → Subscriptions
  3. Find PlanBot in your subscriptions list
  4. Tap Manage
  5. Select Request a refund
  6. Choose a reason and submit

Method 2: Through Google Play Website

  1. Go to play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Find PlanBot subscription
  4. Click Manage → Request a refund
  5. Follow the prompts to complete your request

Method 3: Contact Google Play Support

  1. Visit Google Play Help Center
  2. Select Contact us
  3. Choose Subscriptions & in-app purchases
  4. Explain your refund request

3. Refund Eligibility

✅ Likely to be Approved:

  • Request made within 48 hours of purchase
  • Accidental purchase
  • Technical issues preventing app usage
  • Unauthorized purchase by a child
  • Duplicate charge

⚠️ May Not be Approved:

  • Request made after 48 hours
  • Subscription already used extensively
  • Previous refund requests for the same app
  • Violation of Terms & Conditions

4. What Happens After Refund

If your refund is approved:

  • Immediate: Your subscription will be cancelled
  • Access: Premium features will be revoked immediately
  • Refund Processing: Typically 3-5 business days to your payment method
  • Account: Your account remains active with Free tier access

5. Subscription Cancellation (No Refund)

If you want to cancel your subscription without requesting a refund:

  • You can cancel anytime from Google Play subscriptions
  • You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period
  • No refund for the remaining time in current period
  • Auto-renewal will be disabled

6. Free Trial Cancellation

If you're on a free trial:

  • Cancel anytime during the trial to avoid charges
  • Must cancel at least 24 hours before trial ends
  • No refund needed if cancelled during trial

7. Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical problems that prevent you from using the app:

  1. First: Contact our support team at support@planbot.app
  2. We'll work to resolve the issue within 24-48 hours
  3. If unresolved, we'll assist you with the refund process
  4. Provide details: device type, OS version, error messages

8. Exceptions and Special Cases

8.1 Unauthorized Purchases

If someone made an unauthorized purchase on your account:

  • Report immediately to Google Play Support
  • Secure your Google account (change password)
  • Enable purchase authentication in Google Play settings

8.2 Billing Errors

If you were charged incorrectly:

  • Contact Google Play Support with transaction details
  • Also notify us at billing@planbot.app
  • We'll investigate and assist with resolution

9. Our Commitment

While we cannot process refunds directly, we commit to:

  • ✅ Assist you with the Google Play refund process
  • ✅ Resolve technical issues promptly
  • ✅ Provide clear information about subscription terms
  • ✅ Respond to support requests within 24 hours
